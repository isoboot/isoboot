The BootSource CRD is now available. Create a BootSource:

  cat <<EOF | kubectl apply -f -
  apiVersion: isoboot.github.io/v1alpha1
  kind: BootSource
  metadata:
    name: ubuntu-24-04
  spec:
    iso:
      url:
        binary: "https://releases.ubuntu.com/noble/ubuntu-24.04.3-live-server-amd64.iso"
        shasum: "https://releases.ubuntu.com/noble/SHA256SUMS"
      path:
        kernel: "/casper/vmlinuz"
        initrd: "/casper/initrd"
  EOF

Verify:

  kubectl get bootsources

For more information, visit: https://github.com/isoboot/isoboot
